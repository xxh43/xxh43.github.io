About

My name is Xianghao Xu, I'm a 4th year Ph.D student at Brown University. My research interests include Computer Graphics, Machine Learning and Computer Vision. I had several research experience at Adobe Research, Disney Research and Autodesk Research. Before becoming a phd Student, I was a Senior Software Engineer at Autodesk. Before that, I received my master degree from ETH Zurich (Swiss Federal Institute of Technology) and received my bachelor degree from Politecnico di Torino (Politecnic University of Turin).
